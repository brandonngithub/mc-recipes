Cake

Description

Unlike most food, the cake cannot be eaten as an item in the hotbar. Before being eaten, it must first be placed on top of a solid block. Placing the cake on a slab also works, as the slab acts like a solid block. The cake instead floats half a block on top of the slab.

Each cake has seven "slices"; each use consumes one slice progressing inward from the west. A single slice restores 2 hunger and 0.4 hunger saturation. Eating all seven slices of a cake restores 14 hunger and 2.8 hunger saturation.

Read more about cake on https://minecraft.fandom.com/wiki/Cake

Ingredients

  • milk
  • sugar
  • eggs
  • wheat

Steps

  1. put milk in slot 1, 2, and 3
  2. put sugar in slot 4, and 6
  3. put an egg in slot 5
  4. put wheat in slot 7, 8, and 9
